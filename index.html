Welcome to My Enoy School

My Enoy School (NS) is a public secondary school in Bangladesh, Comilla. It serves students in grades 9–12 in the southern part of the Bangladesh School District, including the neighborhoods of Eastgate, Factoria, Newport Hills, Newport Shores, Somerset, The Summit, and Sunset. As of the 2014-2015 school year, the principal is Dion Yahoudy. The mascot is the Knight, and the school colors are scarlet and gold.
